OUR MISSION

The University of Quy Nhon is a multidisciplinary higher education institution with a mission to train and develop high-quality human resources, nurture talents, conduct scientific research, disseminate knowledge, and transfer technology effectively to contribute to the sustainable development of the country, especially in the South Central - Central Highlands region, and to play a role in advancing social progress.

VISION
By 2030, Quy Nhon University will become a reputable multidisciplinary and application-oriented university, meeting the quality standards of the Southeast Asian region. It will hold a significant position in training, research, academic exchange, and cultural interactions both nationally and internationally.
